Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add CEOs to my watchlist?

Simply search for a CEO using the search function in the app, then tap the "Add to Watchlist" button on their profile page. Your watchlist is automatically saved to your account.

How do I remove CEOs from my watchlist?

Go to your watchlist, find the CEO you want to remove, and tap the "Remove" button next to their name. The change will be saved automatically.

How are CEO ratings calculated?

Our rating methodology combines multiple data sources and metrics. For detailed information about how we calculate ratings, please visit our Methodology page.

Why can't I find a specific CEO?

CEORater is focused on NASDAQ 100 company CEOs.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information and watchlist data are protected using industry-standard security measures. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and data from within the app.

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems with the app, please try these steps:

  • Close and restart the app
  • Check that you have the latest version installed
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Restart your device if the issue persists

If these steps don't resolve the issue, please contact us with details about the problem, including your device model and iOS version.
